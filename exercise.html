Exercise 01

  • What is the difference between var and let?
    • Answer - var is function scoped whereas let is block scoped. Var can be redeclared, but let can't
  • What is the difference between var and const?
    • Answer - var is function scoped whereas const is block scoped. Var can be redeclared, but const can't
  • What is the difference between let and const?
    • Answer - let is can be reassigned a new value, but const can't be redeclared or reassigned
  • What is hoisting?
    • Answer - "JavaScript Hoisting refers to the process whereby the interpreter appears to move the declaration of functions, variables, classes, or imports to the 'top' of their scope, prior to execution of the code." (Hoisting, MDN)
